About the area

A convenient branch for locals and travellers

Gyeldhoung is located in Upper Panbari, Tading Gewog, Samtse Dzongkhag. The branch brings together the restaurant, nearby general shop, and other services so visitors can quickly find what they need in one place.

The restaurant serves buffet meals, fast food, curries, tea, coffee, and drinks. The general shop beside it is useful for daily necessities and essential items. More branch details can be added here as new services become available.
