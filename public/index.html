Welcome

Gyeldhoung Restaurant is located in Upper Panbari, Tading Gewog, Samtse Dzongkhag, offering a comfortable and welcoming dining experience for locals and travelers alike.

We operate a buffet system that gives customers the flexibility to choose how much they want to eat and what dishes they prefer, ensuring both satisfaction and value. Alongside our buffet, we also serve a variety of fast food items, which can be ordered directly through our attentive waiters.

For added convenience, a general shop is located right beside the restaurant, where customers can purchase daily necessities and essential items.

At Gyeldhoung Restaurant, we focus on quality food, flexibility, and customer comfort, making it an ideal place for casual meals, family dining, and group visits.
